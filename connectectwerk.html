Welcome to WERK Kenya

Women Educational Researchers of Kenya (WERK) is a national membership NGO committed to education, gender equity, and social justice. Through research, evidence, and advocacy, we work to bridge policy and practice in Kenya and beyond.

We believe education changes everything. Our digital platform connects you to:

  • Girls' inspiring journeys in education
  • Powerful gender-responsive research
  • Youth empowerment through sports & innovation
  • Community-driven educational transformation

Join us in shaping a brighter, more equal future—where every girl can dream, learn, and lead.
